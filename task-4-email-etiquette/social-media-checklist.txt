Social Media Do’s and Don’ts
Do’s
Use professional and respectful language in posts and comments
Keep your profile updated with accurate information
Share useful, educational, and positive content
Respect others’ opinions and maintain good behavior online
Protect your personal information and privacy
Don’ts
Do not share passwords or sensitive personal details
Avoid posting inappropriate or offensive content
Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
Avoid spreading fake news or unverified information
Do not misuse social media for harmful activities
