# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone (even if they claim to be from a bank)
- Always verify UPI payment requests before approving them
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links received via SMS, email, or social media
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for extra security
- Install apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history
- Be cautious of fake calls or messages claiming rewards or urgent action
- Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches
